## 📖 Project Overview

This project was completed as part of the Digital Literacy course. The objective of this project is to understand and promote safe, effective, and responsible use of digital tools and platforms. As a Student Digital Ambassador, the project focuses on creating awareness among peers about digital literacy, professional online presence, communication etiquette, and cyber safety.

---

## 🧩 Tasks Summary

### 🔹 Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic

Created a one-page infographic using Canva to explain key concepts such as digital literacy, safe internet practices, useful digital tools, and professional online presence.

### 🔹 Task 2 – Digital Portfolio

Created and updated profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle to build a professional digital presence and showcase skills.

### 🔹 Task 3 – Platforms Exploration

Developed a Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz using Google Forms and explored response collection using Google Sheets.

### 🔹 Task 4 – Email Etiquette

Drafted professional emails and created a checklist for responsible social media usage.

### 🔹 Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness

Prepared a case study on phishing and created a prevention checklist to promote safe online practices.
