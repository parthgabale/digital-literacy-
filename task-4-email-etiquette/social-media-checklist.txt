### ✅ Do’s

* Use a professional profile picture and bio.
* Think before posting or sharing any content.
* Protect your privacy by adjusting account settings.
* Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
* Use social media for learning, networking, and personal growth.

### ❌ Don’ts

* Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly.
* Avoid posting offensive, inappropriate, or harmful content.
* Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments.
* Avoid accepting friend requests from unknown people.
* Do not click on suspicious links or scams.
