### 🔒 Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

* Always verify the sender before clicking on any email or message links.
* Do not share passwords, OTPs, or personal information with anyone.
* Check website URLs carefully before entering sensitive details.
* Avoid clicking on suspicious or unknown links.
* Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts.
* Keep your devices and apps updated with the latest security patches.
* Do not use public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.
* Regularly monitor your bank account for unusual activity.

### 💳 UPI / Financial Safety Tips

* Never share your UPI PIN with anyone.
* Always double-check payment requests before approving them.
